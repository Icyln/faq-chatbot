Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?

Once your order has been shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number on our website or the carrier's website to track your package in real-time.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unused items in their original packaging. Simply initiate a return through your account or contact our customer service team for assistance.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to over 100 countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary depending on the destination. You can see the shipping costs at checkout before completing your purchase.

How do I use the AI Style Assistant?

Our AI Style Assistant is available through the chat icon on our website. Simply start a conversation, answer a few questions about your style preferences, and receive personalized fashion recommendations.
